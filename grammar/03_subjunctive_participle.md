# Week 3: 仮定法 + 分詞構文

## 3.1 仮定法

### 解説

仮定法 = **現実と違うこと**を仮定する表現。日本語の「もし〜だったら」。

### 仮定法過去（今の現実と違う仮定）

**形: If + 主語 + 過去形, 主語 + would/could/might + 原形**

| 現実 | 仮定法 |
|------|--------|
| I don't have enough money. | If I **had** enough money, I **would** travel around the world. |
| She isn't here. | If she **were** here, she **could** help us with dinner. |
| We can't take a day off. | If we **could** take a day off, we **would** go hiking. |

**注意:** be動詞は主語に関係なく **were** を使う
- If I **were** you, I would accept the job offer.
- If he **were** available, we would invite him to the party.

### 仮定法過去完了（過去の現実と違う仮定）

**形: If + 主語 + had + 過去分詞, 主語 + would have + 過去分詞**

| 現実（過去） | 仮定法過去完了 |
|-------------|--------------|
| I didn't bring an umbrella. | If I **had brought** an umbrella, I **wouldn't have gotten** soaked. |
| We took the highway. | If we **hadn't taken** the highway, we **would have avoided** the traffic jam. |

### I wish ...

| 今の願望 | I wish I **spoke** French fluently. (話せたらなぁ) |
|---------|-----|
| 過去の後悔 | I wish I **had studied** abroad when I was in college. (留学していたらなぁ) |
| 苛立ち | I wish this rain **would** stop soon. |

## 3.2 分詞構文

### 解説

分詞構文 = **接続詞+主語を省略**して文を短くする。書き言葉・フォーマルで多い。

### 現在分詞 (-ing): 同時・理由・条件

| 元の文 | 分詞構文 |
|--------|---------|
| **Because** I didn't know the way, I got lost. | **Not knowing** the way, I got lost. |
| **While** he was walking through the park, he found a lost wallet. | **Walking** through the park, he found a lost wallet. |
| **If** you take this shortcut, you can save 10 minutes. | **Taking** this shortcut, you can save 10 minutes. |

### 過去分詞 (-ed): 受動の意味

| 元の文 | 分詞構文 |
|--------|---------|
| **Because** it was made from fresh ingredients, the soup tasted wonderful. | **Made** from fresh ingredients, the soup tasted wonderful. |
| **Because** the hotel was located near the beach, it was very popular. | **Located** near the beach, the hotel was very popular. |

### Having + 過去分詞: 先に起きたこと

| 元の文 | 分詞構文 |
|--------|---------|
| **After** I finished my homework, I went out to play. | **Having finished** my homework, I went out to play. |
| **After** we packed all our bags, we headed to the airport. | **Having packed** all our bags, we headed to the airport. |

### 現在分詞 vs 過去分詞の使い分け

- **-ing**: 主語が動作をする側 → "The woman **carrying** a large bag..."
- **-ed**: 主語が動作を受ける側 → "The letter **written** in French..."

### 練習問題 3-2

分詞構文に書き換えなさい。

1. Because I didn't understand the menu, I asked the waiter for help.
2. After she had finished her presentation, she answered questions from the audience.
3. While he was jogging in the park, he ran into an old friend.
4. Because the cake was baked at a low temperature, it stayed moist inside.
5. If you turn left at the traffic light, you can find the post office.

分詞構文を普通の文に戻しなさい。

6. Having checked the weather forecast, we decided to go camping.
7. Not knowing anyone at the party, he stood alone by the door.
8. Built over 100 years ago, the bridge is still in use today.

現在分詞 (-ing) か過去分詞 (-ed) を選びなさい。

9. The cookies (bake) _______ by my grandmother were the best I've ever tasted.
10. The girl (sit) _______ next to me on the train was reading a mystery novel.
11. The letter (write) _______ in French was hard to understand for me.
12. The river (flow) _______ through the town provides drinking water to thousands.

---

## 解答

### 3-1
1. had / would drive
2. were / would not have
3. knew / would send
4. would join / had
5. were / could go
6. had checked / would not have forgotten
7. had left / would have caught
8. would not have been ruined / had not rained
9. were
10. had booked

### 3-2
分詞構文に:
1. Not understanding the menu, I asked the waiter for help.
2. Having finished her presentation, she answered questions from the audience.
3. Jogging in the park, he ran into an old friend.
4. Baked at a low temperature, the cake stayed moist inside.
5. Turning left at the traffic light, you can find the post office.

普通の文に:
6. After we had checked the weather forecast, we decided to go camping.
7. Because he didn't know anyone at the party, he stood alone by the door.
8. Because the bridge was built over 100 years ago, it is still in use today.

-ing / -ed:
9. baked (過去分詞 — クッキーは「焼かれた」側)
10. sitting (現在分詞 — 女の子は「座っている」側)
11. written (過去分詞 — 手紙は「書かれた」側)
12. flowing (現在分詞 — 川は「流れている」側)
